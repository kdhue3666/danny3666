Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 24, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Sudoku handles information when you use the mobile app. Sudoku is a puzzle game that provides game progress, settings, statistics, hints, daily challenges, and ads.

Information We Collect

We do not require users to create an account, and we do not directly collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, contacts, precise location, photos, or payment information.

The app may store the following information locally on your device:

  • Game settings, such as language, sound, and vibration preferences.
  • Game progress, statistics, achievements, streaks, and best times.
  • Daily challenge progress and reward status.
  • Privacy consent status.
  • Ad reward counters, such as completed games since the last interstitial ad.

Advertising

Sudoku uses Google AdMob to show ads, including rewarded ads and interstitial ads. Google and its partners may collect or receive information from the app and your device to provide ads, measure ad performance, prevent fraud, and improve ad services.

This information may include device identifiers, advertising identifiers, IP address, approximate location derived from IP address, app interaction information, crash or diagnostic data, and ad interaction data. These practices are governed by Google's policies and privacy terms.

You can learn more about how Google uses information from apps that use its services at https://policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites .

How We Use Information

Information handled by the app is used to:

  • Save game settings and progress.
  • Show statistics, achievements, and daily challenge results.
  • Provide rewarded hints after eligible ad views.
  • Show ads and measure ad performance through Google AdMob.
  • Maintain app reliability and improve the game experience.

Sharing of Information

We do not sell personal information. The app uses Google AdMob, and Google may process information as described in its own policies. We may also disclose information if required by law or to protect the rights, safety, or security of users, the app, or others.

Data Storage and Retention

Game progress and settings are stored locally on your device. You can delete local game data from the app settings by using the reset data option, or by uninstalling the app. Data handled by Google AdMob is retained according to Google's own retention practices.

Children's Privacy

Sudoku is not directed to children under 13. If you believe that a child has provided personal information through the app, please contact us so we can review the issue.

Your Choices

You can decline the app's privacy consent prompt. If you decline, the app will close on Android and you will not be able to continue using the app. You may also reset local app data from the settings screen.

Security

We use reasonable measures to protect app data. However, no method of electronic storage or transmission is completely secure.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page with a new "Last updated" date.
